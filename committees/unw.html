United Nations Women (UNW)

Overview

The United Nations Women (UNW) is dedicated to gender equality and the empowerment of women. It advocates for women's rights and works to eliminate discrimination and violence against women globally.

Key Topics

  • Gender-Based Violence and Its Prevention
  • Women's Economic Empowerment
  • Representation of Women in Leadership
  • Access to Education and Healthcare for Women

Objectives

By the end of the session, delegates will aim to:

  • Draft resolutions promoting gender equality in all sectors.
  • Discuss strategies to combat violence against women.
  • Enhance collaboration between member states on women's rights initiatives.

Chairperson

Ms. Aisha Noor - A leading advocate for women's rights with extensive experience in international development and policy-making.
