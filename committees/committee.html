Committees Overview

At RMMUN, we strive to simulate the real-world workings of the United Nations by providing a platform for students to engage in meaningful discussions across various global issues. Each committee focuses on specific topics, allowing delegates to dive deep into international relations and diplomacy.

UNCSTD

United Nations Conference on Science and Technology for Development

This conference is designed to facilitate discussions on how science and technology can be utilized to support development goals, especially in developing countries.

International Court of Justice

In MUN, the ICJ focuses on resolving fictional or historical legal disputes between states and providing advisory opinions on pressing legal questions.

ICJ

UNSC

United Nation Security Council

The Security Council is responsible for maintaining international peace and security. This committee addresses urgent issues that threaten global stability

Human Rights Council

The Human Rights Council promotes and protects human rights around the globe. This committee addresses violations and advocates for fundamental freedoms

HRC

PNA

Pakistan National Assembly

The PNA committee provides delegates with the opportunity to engage in discussions that mirror the complexities of Pakistani governance and legislative procedures.

United Nations Women

United Nations Women (UNW) is dedicated to achieving gender equality and empowering women and girls globally.

UNW
