Pakistan National Assembly (PNA)

Overview

The Pakistan National Assembly (PNA) is the lower house of the Parliament of Pakistan. It plays a crucial role in formulating laws and representing the interests of the citizens of Pakistan.

Key Topics

  • Legislative Reforms and Policy Making
  • Social Justice and Welfare Programs
  • Economic Development Strategies
  • Foreign Relations and Diplomacy

Objectives

By the end of the session, delegates will aim to:

  • Draft legislation addressing key national issues.
  • Engage in debates on current socio-economic challenges.
  • Formulate strategies for effective governance and accountability.
