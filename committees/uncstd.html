United Nations Committee on Science and Technology for Development (UNCSTD)

Overview

The UNCSTD addresses the intersection of science, technology, and development. It focuses on leveraging technological advancements to promote sustainable development globally.

Key Topics

  • Innovation for Sustainable Development
  • Technology Transfer and Capacity Building
  • Digital Divide and Access to Technology
  • Impact of Emerging Technologies on Development

Objectives

By the end of the session, delegates will aim to:

  • Propose strategies for integrating technology into development policies.
  • Discuss ways to enhance international cooperation on scientific research.
  • Draft recommendations for addressing the digital divide.
